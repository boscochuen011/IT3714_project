About The Elf wars

Game Story


The elf homeland was invaded and expelled by monsters for 100 years ago. The elf awakened to the power of fire from the anger. In order to take back their homeland, they are entering the journey of defeating monsters.


Game Overview


Players must go through different levels until they defeat the final boss to beat the game.

Game Character and Props

Elf

controlled by player and shotting projectile to deal damage to monster and boss.

Boss

move around and heavy damage projectile deal damage to player.

Monster

move around and deal damage to player

Heart

Diamond

Game control

Movement

W(up) S(Down) A(Left) D(Right)

Attack

Mouse left (Attack) Mouse slide (Direction)
